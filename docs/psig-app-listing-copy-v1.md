## Store Description Draft

### English

PSI-G is a spiritual guidance app built for people who want more than a timer.

Inside PSI-G, meditation is connected to a living journey:

- Grow your Light Orb through daily practice
- Generate personalized AI meditation sessions
- Explore the Core Journey inside the meditation library
- Send blessings and orb gifts to others
- Track your progress, resonance, and awakening path

PSI-G is designed to feel calm, intentional, and symbolic. Instead of treating meditation like a checklist, it turns practice into an unfolding inner journey.

Best for:

- people who want guided meditation with more meaning
- users who enjoy ritual, spiritual reflection, and symbolic progress
- people who want both structured courses and personalized AI sessions

### Traditional Chinese

PSI-G 並不是一個只用來倒數的冥想計時器。

在 PSI-G 裡，冥想會連接成一段真正的靈性旅程：

- 向他人送出祝福與光球禮物
- 追蹤進度、Resonance 與覺醒路徑

PSI-G 的設計重點是安靜、儀式感與象徵感。它不把冥想當成待辦清單，而是把每次練習變成一段持續展開的內在成長。

適合：

- 想要更有意義的引導式冥想的人
