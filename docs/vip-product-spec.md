# VIP Product Spec

## Positioning

PSI-G VIP is a 30-day spiritual pass.

It should sell three things:

- Full access to the ambient sound library
- Faster orb growth and earlier access to beautiful orb forms
- A stronger sense of identity, ritual, and progress

VIP should not replace gameplay.  
Free users can still grow, awaken, and unlock forms.  
VIP should feel like acceleration and enrichment, not a hard paywall on the core app.

## VIP Duration

- VIP duration: 30 days
- Show:
  - activated date
  - expiry date
  - remaining days
- When expired:
  - remove VIP-only access
  - keep user progress, history, and unlocked non-VIP content
  - do not delete personal data or meditation records

## Ambient Sound Access

### Free Tier

- Unlock 5 selected ambient soundscapes
- The free set should cover:
  - ocean / water
  - temple / bell
  - crystal resonance
  - soft stream
  - one clean frequency option

### VIP Tier

- Unlock all ambient sound options
- Includes:
  - deeper temple / ritual ambiences
  - more healing frequencies
  - richer nature soundscapes
  - premium ceremonial textures

## Orb Growth System

## Seven Chakra Awakening Concept

The seven-day awakening journey should be framed as a chakra practice.

Core concept:

- the user begins with seven chakra meditations across seven days
- each day corresponds to one chakra
- each meditation day helps align, heal, or activate that chakra
- after seven days, the seven energies integrate into one awakened orb

This gives the awakening loop a deeper meaning than just "7 days of usage".

Recommended chakra mapping:

- Day 1: Root Chakra
  - grounding
  - safety
  - red
- Day 2: Sacral Chakra
  - emotion
  - creativity
  - orange
- Day 3: Solar Plexus Chakra
  - confidence
  - will
  - yellow
- Day 4: Heart Chakra
  - compassion
  - love
  - green
- Day 5: Throat Chakra
  - expression
  - truth
  - blue
- Day 6: Third Eye Chakra
  - intuition
  - insight
  - indigo
- Day 7: Crown Chakra
  - transcendence
  - spiritual connection
  - violet

Product framing:

- every meditation day nourishes one chakra color / energy state
- the user sees progress as color, energy, and ritual rather than abstract streak numbers
- after the seventh chakra is completed, the app rewards the user with one awakened orb

Clarification:

- free users and VIP users both complete all 7 chakra days
- the spiritual arc must remain complete for both tiers
- VIP changes daily effort, not the number of chakras in the awakening path

Suggested user-facing copy direction:

- "Complete seven chakra meditations across seven days to awaken your orb."
- "Each day aligns one chakra. When all seven resonate together, your awakened orb is born."
- "Your awakened orb is the union of seven aligned energies."

### Base Rule

- Every user starts with a normal orb
- A normal orb becomes an awakened orb after:
  - 7 days of meditation streak

### Daily Requirement

- Free user:
  - 7 minutes per day
  - for 7 days
- VIP user:
  - 5 minutes per day
  - for 7 days

Important:

- VIP does not reduce the 7-day journey
- VIP only reduces the daily effort
- This preserves the ritual and growth fantasy
- The seven-day structure should be explained as a seven-chakra awakening ritual, not only as a streak mechanic

## VIP Instant Reward

When a user buys VIP:

- Instantly grant 1 gifted awakened orb

Why:

- The user gets immediate delight
- They can explore shape transformation right away without erasing the meaning of the main 7-day journey
- The long-term orb growth loop still matters for future orbs

## Orb Form Unlock Philosophy

Orb forms should not be fully sold through payment alone.

Recommended model:

- Some forms are gifted by VIP immediately
- Most forms are unlocked by missions
- Free and VIP users both use the mission system
- VIP users complete missions faster or with lower thresholds

## VIP Instant Shape Rewards

VIP should instantly grant 3 to 5 premium forms.

Recommended starter VIP forms:

- `quantum-orbitals`
- `halo-bloom`
- `ring-torus`
- `cosmic-serpent`
- `vortex-ring`

Reason:

- They feel visually premium
- They give instant value
- They do not destroy the long-term unlock loop for every other shape

## Shape Unlock Structure

### Immediately Available

- normal orb base form
- a very small starter set of simple forms after awakening

### VIP Gifted

- 3 to 5 premium forms granted immediately on VIP purchase

### Mission Unlocks

- most sacred geometry and advanced forms
- celestial / galaxy forms
- rare ritual forms

## Mission Design Principles

Missions should not feel like chores.

They should express four themes:

- habit
- ritual
- exploration
- connection
- inner energy development

The user should feel they are living a spiritual journey, not farming KPI.

## Mission Categories

### 1. Habit Missions

- Complete meditation at the same time for 3 days
- Finish a morning meditation streak
- Finish a sleep meditation streak
- Meditate 7 days in a row
- Complete one chakra meditation on schedule

### 1b. Chakra Missions

- Complete your Root Chakra meditation
- Complete your Heart Chakra meditation
- Finish all seven chakra meditations
- Balance three chakras in one week
- Reach Crown Chakra and awaken your first orb

### 2. Exploration Missions

- Try 3 different meditation categories
- Use 3 different orb states
- Switch to a new orb form for the first time
- Finish your first premium-style guided lesson

### 3. Ritual Missions

- Complete a sunrise ritual
- Complete a before-sleep ritual
- Enter diffuse state and hold it during a session
- Complete a weekly reflection session

### 4. Connection Missions

- Send a light orb gift
- Receive a gift
- Complete your first blessing transmission
- Connect with 2 different friends

## Mission Naming Style

Do not present missions like generic game tasks.

Prefer names like:

- Seven-Day Awakening
- Sevenfold Awakening
- Dawn Alignment
- Night of Stillness
- First Blessing
- Orb of Resonance
- Moonlit Breath
- Temple of Silence
- Spiral of Return

## Free vs VIP Mission Tuning

VIP should not skip missions completely.

VIP should receive lighter thresholds.

Examples:

- Free:
  - meditate 7 minutes x 7 days
- VIP:
  - meditate 5 minutes x 7 days

- Free:
  - send 3 gifts
- VIP:
  - send 2 gifts

- Free:
  - finish 5 sessions of a category
- VIP:
  - finish 3 sessions of the same category

Target reduction:

- reduce mission effort by around 20% to 30%

## VIP Benefits Summary

VIP should unlock:

- all ambient sounds
- 1 instant gifted awakened orb
- 3 to 5 premium forms immediately
- reduced daily meditation minutes for orb growth
- lower mission thresholds
- VIP identity treatment in profile / garden / gifting

## Not Recommended

Avoid these:

- unlocking every single form immediately via VIP
- making awakening possible only by payment
- deleting user progress on expiry
- reducing the 7-day arc to almost nothing
- making free users feel blocked from the main fantasy

Free users should feel:

- "I can still grow this spiritual orb through devotion."

VIP users should feel:

- "I gained full content, beautiful forms, and a faster sacred journey."
